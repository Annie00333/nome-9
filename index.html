Welcome Adventurer

Step into the medieval world of Heldorya, where ancient castles rise above sprawling biomes and epic adventures await. Valdareth, the grand castle, is your home base for quests, building, and battles. Join a community of brave players and explore a modded Minecraft experience like no other.

Everything You Need to Know

Heldorya is a Forge 1.20.1 modded server with carefully chosen mods to enhance your gameplay, from magical ores to fierce dragons. Our unique features and immersive builds invite you to live the medieval fantasy fully.

Laws of the Land

  • Respect all players and admins; hostility will not be tolerated.
  • Griefing and stealing are strictly forbidden and punishable by confinement in the castle cells.
  • Use of exploits or hacks will result in immediate banishment.
  • Housing is limited to purchased homes in the village spawn area.
  • Be mindful of the environment and fellow adventurers.
  • To enter the server, contact admins first for mod verification and whitelisting.
